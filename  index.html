Doge (2013)

The Doge meme became famous in 2013 using a picture of a Shiba Inu dog with colorful Comic Sans text. It became one of the internet's most recognizable memes and even inspired Dogecoin which was a powerful coin.

Drake Hotline Bling

Based on Drake's music video, this meme is used to compare things people dislike and things they prefer. It remains one of the most popular comparison meme formats to ever be available.

Distracted Boyfriend

Taken from a stock photo, this meme exploded in 2017. It represents someone ignoring one thing for something new as the old (woman) is right fully irritated by the man

Success Kid

A picture of a toddler holding sand became the symbol of small victories and unexpected success making it a nice metaphor.

Woman Yelling At Cat

Combining two unrelated images created one of the funniest internet arguments ever, best for anything.

Roll Safe

This meme comes from a web series and is used to represent terrible logic presented as genius, just like modern problems require modern solutions.

Change My Mind

Steven Crowder sitting behind a table became a worldwide meme used to invite debates.

Surprised Pikachu

A screenshot from Pokémon became the perfect reaction for predictable outcomes.

Stonks

The businessman named Meme Man represents hilariously bad financial decisions.

This Is Fine

Originating from the comic Gunshow, the dog calmly sits in a burning room represents ignoring chaos.
